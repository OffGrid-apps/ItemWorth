Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your data stays on your device

ItemWorth is designed with your privacy as a first principle. Your inventory data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and never leaves your device.

What we never do

  • Collect or transmit personal information
  • Require an account or sign-up
  • Upload your inventory data to any server
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Display advertisements
  • Share data with any third party

Data storage

All inventory data is stored using your browser's localStorage API. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app will permanently delete your inventory. We recommend keeping your own backup for important items.
