ItemWorth

Version 1.0.0

A private, offline-first personal inventory tracker. Organize, value, and manage everything you own — no account, no cloud, no tracking.

Features

  • Works completely offline — no internet required
  • Installable as a PWA on any device
  • All data stored locally on your device
  • Create, edit, and organize inventory items
  • Track estimated value across your collection
  • Category and location tracking
  • No account, no signup, no data collection
